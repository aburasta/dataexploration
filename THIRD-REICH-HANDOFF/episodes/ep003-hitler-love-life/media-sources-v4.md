# ep003 — Media Sources v4 (VIDEO-DOMINANT re-source, 2026-07-18)

Re-sources the media the handoff intentionally left behind, rebuilt as a **video-dominant** cut:
motion leads every beat that has real archival footage; stills are now the *supporting* layer (documents,
portraits, and the sanctioned callbacks) rather than the backbone. Maximum variety — no incidental
repeats; only the two deliberate callbacks recur. Organized by the `render-spec-v3-notes.md` P01–P18
scene map. **Sourcing only — render/timing instructions come separately.**

**Status legend:** `✓listed` = exact source page returned/confirmed reachable in research ·
`◇check` = collection confirmed, exact clip/file still to pick & rights-confirm at download ·
`✋no-face` = no clearable portrait exists — cover with context, do not fabricate.

**Rights tiers:** `PD-US` (pre-1929 / US-gov) · `NARA` (US-seized Nazi property, generally PD) ·
`BArch` (Bundesarchiv, **CC-BY-SA 3.0 DE — must credit**) · `EDIT` (editorial/fair-use, e.g. book covers).

> **Bundesarchiv caveat on US-gov compilation footage:** PD US-gov reels like *The Nazi Plan* and
> Universal Newsreels are PD in the US, but some carry German newsreel whose underlying rights the
> Bundesarchiv asserts (esp. for European broadcast). Where a clip is BArch-origin, add the credit.

## D. Occult montage (P02) — kept, with a sharper presentation
**Keep the fast book-cover montage**, but present it as *"the interpretive angle this video rejects"*:
- Flash real cover art of ~5 books — *The Nazis and the Occult* (Sklar), *The Nazi Occult* (Hite,
  Osprey), *Gods & Beasts: The Nazis and the Occult* (Sklar), + 2 similar — ~1.5s each, with a fast
  push on each so even the montage moves (fits the video-dominant cut). Source covers from
  publisher/retailer listings. `EDIT`/fair-use.
- **Better framing (recommended):** hard-cut *out* of the last cover straight onto a warm, human
  Hitler/Geli image exactly as the narration turns — "This video takes a different view. Love and
  passion are core human affections…". The covers = *other people's occult reading*; the cut to a
  human face = *the angle we actually take*. Makes the pivot a visual argument, not just B-roll.
- Presenting the covers as **books/discourse** (not literal occult imagery) is deliberate: it frames
  the occult reading as other authors' theories rather than visually endorsing it. Avoid real
  "occult ritual" footage here — it would undercut the very point the narration is making.
