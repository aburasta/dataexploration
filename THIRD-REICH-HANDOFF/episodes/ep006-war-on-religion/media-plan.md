## Open sourcing risks (flagged now, before any browsing)
- **Tag 2 (Krause):** minor public figure — a clean portrait may not exist. Fallback: a Sportpalast
  podium/speaker shot without positive ID, captioned honestly.
- **Tag 33 (1936 memorandum) / Tag 48 (Bormann circular) / Tag 49 (Rosenberg's 30 points):** these
  are real documents referenced in the book/script, but I have not confirmed public-domain scans
  exist. Default to a generic, honestly-generic period document/typed page rather than fabricating
  a specific one — same rule ep003 used for the Heiden letter (img8A, "deliberately unreadable").
- **Tag 54 (cross replaced by swastika):** Rosenberg's National Reich Church was never actually
  implemented, so there's no literal photo of a German church with its cross replaced by a
  swastika. Using a real swastika-on-church-building photo as a symbolic stand-in, not a literal
  depiction of point 19 being carried out — will caption this distinction to you at review.
- **Tags 35/41/42 (concentration camps):** sensitivity rule applies — sober, non-graphic exteriors
  only, consistent with the runbook's content-based sensitivity rule.
