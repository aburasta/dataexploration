No image or shot appears twice. Every one of the ~58 beats below gets its own unique asset. Where
one newsreel/photo-set has several usable moments, each distinct shot is cut separately and

separate Mussolini portraits, Hitler portraits, Blackshirt/Brownshirt shots, and meeting photos.

## ⚠️ SENSITIVITY — no atrocity / no corpse imagery (house rule, critical this episode)
This script contains executions. **We do not show bodies.** Specifically:
- **Piazzale Loreto / "strung up by the heels"** (opening beat 3 and closing beat 54): **never** the
  hanging corpses or the crowd defiling them. Use only sober stand-ins — the *place* (the Milan
  square / the Esso station forecourt as it looks), or the partisan capture, or a period crowd shot
  from behind — and caption honestly. The two mentions get **two different** sober images (zero-repeat).
- **Dollfuss murder, Abyssinia war, POW columns, bombings:** sober framing only — portraits,
  buildings, troops on the march, prisoners walking (no dead, no wounded, no suffering).
- Nazi/Fascist insignia incidental, never the hero of the frame.
